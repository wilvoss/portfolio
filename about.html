My career began as a pre-production artist in Massachusetts where I cut my design teeth working for Bechtel/Parsons Brinckerhoff on the Big Dig (the Central Artery Project) in downtown Boston. My artistic skills led to further design work in Boston and then New Orleans, Louisiana, where I designed and produced collateral for local companies that included the Louisiana Philharmonic Orchestra, Commander's Palace, Palace Cafe, Brennan's, and the New Orleans International Airport.

My family now calls Seattle home where I have been building a body of work that represents game design, UX consulting, prototyping, branding, web development, and integration for clients that include XBox Accessories, Microsoft Hardware UX, Avanade, Microsoft IT, MSN, Bing, Washington Tourism, Washington State Wine Commission, Columbia Bank, Skagit Bank, and Captaris.
